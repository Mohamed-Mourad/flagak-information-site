About

FLAGAK is an Egyptian mobile application dedicated to celebrating and supporting local brands. We bring together the best of Egyptian vendors under one unified mobile application, offering an extensive range of products across 10 diverse categories. Our goal is to cater to the unique tastes and needs of customers while fostering a space where local craftsmanship and innovation thrive.

Vendors

FLAGAK empowers Egyptian businesses by providing an intuitive and user-friendly platform that simplifies the selling process. Vendors can easily upload their products, manage orders efficiently, and connect with a broad audience of local shoppers. Beyond technology, we offer tools, resources, and insights to help vendors grow their businesses and establish a strong presence in the market. We’re here to ensure that every Egyptian vendor has the opportunity to succeed in the competitive e-commerce landscape.

Shoppers

At FLAGAK, we are redefining the shopping experience by bringing authentic Egyptian products directly to your fingertips. With a wide variety of items from trusted local vendors, shoppers can enjoy a seamless and secure purchasing journey. Our platform also offers convenient consumer financing options to make quality local goods more accessible. By choosing FLAGAK, you unlock the easiest and most convenient way to shop for authentic Egyptian products.

Mission

Our mission is to create a vibrant and inclusive ecosystem that uplifts Egyptian vendors while making local shopping easy, enjoyable, and meaningful. By connecting local businesses with wider audiences, we aim to showcase the beauty and quality of Egyptian craftsmanship on a national and global scale.
